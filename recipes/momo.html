MOMOS

Ingredients list

  • Plain Flour
  • Cabbage/Meat
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Spices

Steps to Make:

  1. Chop the Vegetables/Meat .
  2. Add Spices and necessary items.
  3. Mix Plain Flour with water and make some small balls with it.
  4. Now use a belan to make it circular and flat.
  5. Fill that with previously chopped Vegetables/Meat
  6. Use Special Utensils and Cook the Momo in Steam for 10-20 minutes
  7. Serve that with a Chutney. Y
  8. Yummy Delicious your meal is ready.
